How to Play Star Collector

Star Collector is an exciting online reaction speed game that tests your reflexes and hand-eye coordination. The objective is simple yet challenging: click on as many falling stars as possible within the 30-second time limit to achieve the highest score.

🎯 Simple Gameplay

Just click on the stars that appear randomly across the game area. Each star gives you points, but they disappear quickly!

⚡ Progressive Difficulty

As you score more points, the game level increases and stars appear and disappear faster, making the challenge more intense.

🏆 Competitive Scoring

Different stars give different point values. Avoid the black stars, as they will deduct points!

📱 Play Anywhere

Fully responsive design works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. No download required!

Tips for High Scores

Master these strategies to maximize your Star Collector performance:

  • Focus on the center of the game area where most stars appear
  • Be careful to avoid the black trap stars!
  • Use quick, precise clicks rather than frantic clicking
  • Prioritize stars that just appeared - they have more time before disappearing
  • Stay calm as the game speeds up in higher levels
  • Practice regularly to improve your reaction time
