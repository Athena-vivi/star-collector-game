Our Story

Star Collector was created with a simple mission: to provide an engaging, accessible, and fun way for people to test and improve their reaction speeds. Inspired by the beauty of starry nights and the excitement of quick reflexes, we designed a game that anyone can enjoy instantly in their browser.

We believe that great games should be simple to understand but challenging to master. Star Collector embodies this philosophy by offering straightforward gameplay mechanics while providing endless opportunities for improvement and competition with yourself.

Technology & Design

Star Collector is built using modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Our game engine is optimized for smooth performance across all devices while maintaining beautiful visual effects and responsive controls.

The game features a carefully crafted user interface designed for accessibility and ease of use. Every element from the star animations to the scoring system has been fine-tuned to provide the best possible gaming experience.

Different Star Types

Star Collector features four unique star types, each with different behaviors and point values:

  • Golden Stars (⭐): Standard stars worth 8 points - your main source of scoring
  • Lightning Stars (⚡): Special blue stars worth 5 points that also add 3 seconds to your timer
  • Rare Stars (🌟): Premium stars worth 25 points - harder to find but very valuable
  • Trap Stars (⚫): Dangerous black stars that subtract 25 points - avoid these!
