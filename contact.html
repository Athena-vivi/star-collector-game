Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I improve my reaction time in Star Collector?
A: Practice regularly, focus on the center of the game area, stay calm as the game speeds up, and prioritize newly appeared stars. Quick, precise clicks work better than frantic clicking.
Q: What do the different star types do?
A: Golden stars (⭐) give 8 points, Lightning stars (⚡) give 5 points plus 3 seconds, Rare stars (🌟) give 25 points, and Trap stars (⚫) subtract 25 points - avoid these!
Q: Is the game really free?
A: Yes! Star Collector is completely free to play with no in-app purchases or premium content. We're supported by advertising revenue, which helps us keep the game free for everyone.
Q: Can I play on mobile devices?
A: Absolutely! Star Collector is fully optimized for mobile devices, tablets, and desktops. The game automatically adapts to your screen size for the best experience.
Q: How does the scoring system work?
A: You earn points by clicking stars before they disappear. Different stars have different values. Your level increases every 150 points, making the game faster and more challenging.
Q: Will you add multiplayer support?
A: We're always considering new features based on community feedback! Multiplayer is an interesting idea that we may explore in future updates.
